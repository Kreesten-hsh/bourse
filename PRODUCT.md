# to the world Product Context

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

`to the world` is a private mobility command center for finding, filtering, scoring, reading, tracking, and applying to funded international opportunities. The product turns raw opportunity feeds into clear decisions: ignore, analyze, prioritize, prepare, apply, or archive.

## Primary User

Kreesten-Eddy Agboton, a 20-year-old computer science student in Cotonou, Benin, preparing for international study, internships, fellowships, trainings, jobs, and funded humanitarian or volunteer programs.

## Hard Constraints

- Budget is zero by default.
- Opportunities must be free, fully funded, partially funded, paid, or provide concrete support.
- Computer science, software, cybersecurity, data, AI, digital transformation, and related technical domains are first priority.
- The system must expose hidden blockers before the user spends time applying.
- Official application links must be preserved and easy to open.
- Every score must be explainable.

## Product Principles

- Clarity before volume: one excellent opportunity is more valuable than 100 noisy links.
- Decision support, not passive bookmarking.
- Official source first, summary second.
- Never present ambiguous funding as confirmed funding.
- Deadline and eligibility are first-class data, not notes.
- The interface must stay calm while the data changes daily.

## Anti References

- Generic scholarship blog.
- Card-heavy SaaS dashboard.
- Dark crypto-style dashboard.
- Decorative AI assistant interface.
- Opportunity list with titles only and no eligibility extraction.

## Success Criteria

- The user understands an opportunity in less than 30 seconds.
- The user can answer: funded, eligible, deadline, documents, direct application link.
- The user receives daily relevant alerts without manually checking each source.
- The system tracks applications from discovery to result.
- Duplicates and expired opportunities do not pollute the workspace.
